Program Control Loop

1. Capture

Collect websites, LinkedIn posts, app records, deployment notes, repo commits, screenshots and public reposts.

2. Classify

Classify every claim as REAL, PARTIAL or BLOCKED. No science claim without source receipts.

3. Map

Connect each observation to research branch, product branch, business branch and funding/IP pathway.

4. Package

Create launch poster, evidence appendix, media Q&A, grant appendix, IP memo and domain/service-catalog entries.

5. Execute

Route tasks to Bridge/workers: crawl, screenshot, ledger, publish, deploy, receipt, retry, escalate only if authority missing.

6. Govern

Keep evidence live: failure becomes spin-off evidence; new evidence moves PARTIAL to REAL; stale claims are demoted.
